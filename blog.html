Ask Arbesko

All Questions Welcomed!

Part of our commitment to our customers is offering help with questions and concerns you have before and while owning our quality footwear. Because of this, we welcome all of your questions regarding our products!

Engineer — Ask our engineer technical questions concerning safety and occupational footwear. Frequently asked questions include allergies, anti-static, ESD, chemicals, materials, standards, protection classes, and more.

Specialist — Ask our specialist questions regarding insoles, footwear, and musculskeletal problems at work and leisure.

Designer — Give our designer your advice and inputs about improvements, colors or ideas about our footwear in general.
